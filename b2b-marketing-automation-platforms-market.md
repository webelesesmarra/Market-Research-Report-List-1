<p><p>B2B marketing automation platforms can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based platforms are hosted on the vendor's servers and accessible via the internet, offering scalability, ease of updates, and lower upfront costs. They enable users to implement campaigns quickly without heavy IT involvement. In contrast, on-premises solutions are installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data and customization but requiring more resources for maintenance and infrastructure, making them suitable for businesses with specific security or compliance needs.</p></p>
